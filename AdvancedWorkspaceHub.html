🔎 Verification Q/A Helper

What is the last modification/change requested by customer?
Go to 360 → Recent Requests & Recent Payments. Check the last modification date.

What is the registered contact email address?
From the left side menu → Contact Email.

Have you contacted Customer Care in the past 30 days?
Go to Interaction → Customer Journey → check the date table.
Also check Interaction → Account → Channels (check the date, within 7 days).
In Customer Journey, filter Interactions Type to CALLS.

Have you transferred any credit in the last 7 days?
Go to Billing → Adjustment and Transfers → UBT Details History → Last 7 Days.

What is the active service or current plan customer is on (Name, Benefits or Rental)?
Go to Profile → Account → Account Services. Check if it's chargeable, one-time, etc.

What is the remaining amount of Benefits/Allowance?
Go to 360 → Usage Summary → IN Services. Check remaining benefits/allowance.
